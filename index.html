The Furry trainer app is designed for training, maintaining health and activity.
It can be used both for morning exercises
and for small warm-UPS during the day and full-fledged training.
In the app, you can configure your training programs or choose any of the suggested ones.
You can change and customize each of your training sessions.
Add or decrease exercises, change their duration and number of repetitions.
